Question 02 • Macro-Economics

Which of the following would cause a leftward shift in the Aggregate Demand curve?

cancel

Your Selection

An increase in government spending INCORRECT

Correct Answer

An increase in household taxes check_circle
Explanation

Aggregate Demand (AD) = C + I + G + (X - M). An increase in government spending (G) shifts the curve to the right. Conversely, an increase in taxes reduces disposable income, thereby reducing Consumption (C) and shifting the AD curve to the left.
